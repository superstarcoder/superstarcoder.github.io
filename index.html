about me







Hello! My name is Dhanish Natarajan. I am a sophomore at Lynbrook High School. I love combining my creativity and problem solving skills to develop programs that solve real world problems. I work on numerous coding projects in my free time to expand my knowledge since there is an endless ocean of concepts, tools, APIs and libraries to learn about. Through my 5 years of programming experience, I have intuitively learnt the way to be an efficient software developer by being organized, following conventions, making my code more readable and much more. I have observed mistakes beginner programmers make and have corrected myself by learning not to repeat those mistakes.

Skills and Interests

I learn new skills by working on computer science projects in diverse fields. This way, I can build upon my knowledge to pursue my passion for developing programs/applications that solve real world problems

Machine Learning

Machine learning never fails to fascinate me because of its potential to solve numerous problems that cannot be solved through regular code. I have some experience with ML related libraries such as tensorflow, pytorch, scikit learn and numpy

Game Development

Game development is where I can combine my programming skills and creativity to enrich my learning through an enjoyable experience. I have developed games with python's graphics library called pygame.

Frontend

I have experience with javascript, html, css, and bootstrap and have learnt what it takes to build a responsive and engaging website frontend for the user

Backend

I have a substantial amount of experience with python and some of its libraries like flask and opencv. I have also used the mongodb and sqlite3 databases in various projects.
